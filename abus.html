Super Manufacturing INC

Is an ISO-Certified Manufacturing Company

A Plant Office mainly distributing small powered Transformers up to 2kVA. Since the establishment in August 1998, Super Manufacturing INC. has significantly grown, providing superior products and services and have achieved significant milestones.

back to home

1958

Established in May of 1958, Super Manufacturing, Inc., started its operation by producing decorative lamps and wall lamps.

1960

The company started manufacturing safety switches and also engaged in distribution of electronic product and has been the exclusive distributor of Kenwood Home and Car Audio Video products.

1970

Expanding its line of electrical products, the company also manufactures panelboards, motor control centers, switchgears and dry type transformers, besides repairing and reconditioning oil immersed transformers.

1983

The Bureau of Product and Standards grants the license to use Quality Certification Mark for the safety switches manufactured by Super Manufacturing, Inc.

1986

In its desire to be in step with its Asian neighbors, Super Manufacturing, Inc., went into a Technical Agreement with Fortune Electric Co. Ltd, a licensee of McGraw Edison of U.S.A. for the manufacturing of oil-immersed transformers. Since then, its established brand in the market, Wagner transformers, continues to gain market demand and popularity with NEA electric cooperatives, private electric companies and contractors nationwide due to its high quality and reliability.

1998

The Transformer Division move to its new plant, a 2 hectare land in Calamba City Laguna. The plant is capable of manufacturing distribution and small power transformers up to 2,000kVA, with rated voltage of 34.5kV and below, with a plant capacity of 80,000kVA per shift per annum.

2001

Super Manufacturing, Inc. was certified to ISO 9002.

2003

ISO 9002 was upgraded to ISO 9001:2000.

2010

ISO 9001:2008.

2016

With the onset of being an ISO certified company and further innovation in management and appropriate business strategies, Super Manufacturing, Inc. in the 21st century will continue to grow as highly dynamic and vigorous enterprise.
